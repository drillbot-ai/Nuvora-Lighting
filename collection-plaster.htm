Material Story

The Poetry of Earth & Water

Plaster speaks the oldest language of architecture—earth and water united by human hands. Our artisans work with natural lime plaster, shaping each fixture by hand to create organic textures that capture and release light in soft, atmospheric waves.

"Plaster remembers the touch of its making. Each surface carries the subtle impressions of the artisan's hands, creating fixtures that feel alive, breathing with the light they hold."

The inherent warmth of plaster creates intimate lighting experiences. Unlike cold metals or perfect glass, plaster fixtures invite interaction—their matte surfaces absorb harsh light and return it as gentle illumination that changes throughout the day, revealing different textures as shadows shift and settle.

Natural Properties

Organic Texture

Natural porosity creates gentle light distribution, eliminating harsh shadows and creating serene ambience.

Natural Aging

Develops beautiful patina over time, becoming more characterful and beautiful with age and exposure.
