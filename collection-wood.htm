Material Story

The Memory of the Forest

Wood is the only material that was once truly alive, and that vitality remains in every grain and knot. Our artisans work with sustainably sourced walnut and oak, honoring each tree's unique growth story while transforming it into lighting that brings warmth and organic beauty to contemporary spaces.

"Every wood fixture carries the memory of seasons—the rush of spring growth, the steady patience of summer, the wisdom of countless winters. Light through wood feels like sunlight through forest canopy."

We allow the natural grain to guide our design, working with rather than against the wood's inherent character. Each piece reveals different patterns as light passes through varying wood density, creating fixtures that change throughout the day as illumination shifts and natural oils develop deeper richness over time.
