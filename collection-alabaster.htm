Material Story

The Stone That Breathes Light

Alabaster possesses a unique gift—the ability to capture light and release it as a gentle, otherworldly glow. Our artisans work with carefully selected alabaster stones, each piece chosen for its translucency and inherent capacity to transform harsh illumination into ethereal presence.

"Alabaster doesn't simply hold light—it transforms it. Each fixture becomes a luminous sculpture, glowing from within like captured moonlight, creating atmosphere that feels both ancient and eternal."

The natural veining and subtle variations in alabaster create fixtures that are never quite the same. Light travels differently through each stone, creating unique patterns and intensities that shift throughout the day, making every alabaster piece a living element that responds to changing illumination.
