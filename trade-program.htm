Common Questions

Trade Program FAQ

How long does the application process take?

Most applications are reviewed and approved within 2-3 business days. Complex applications may take up to 5 business days for verification.

Can I upgrade my tier level later?

Yes, tier levels are reviewed annually based on purchase volume. You can also request a tier review if your business grows significantly during the year.

Are there minimum order requirements?

No minimum orders for Professional tier. Professional Plus requires $500 minimum. VIP Partners have flexible terms based on annual commitments.

What payment terms are available?

Professional tier: Credit card or NET 15. Professional Plus: NET 30 terms available. VIP Partners: Custom payment terms based on relationship.
