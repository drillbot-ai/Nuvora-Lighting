Proper care ensures your NUVORA fixtures maintain their natural beauty and develop character gracefully over time. Follow our comprehensive guide for material-specific preservation.

Material Care

Care by Material Type

Natural Plaster

Earthen material requiring gentle care

  • Dust gently with soft natural bristle brush
  • Avoid water exposure - plaster breathes naturally
  • Use dry microfiber cloth for spot cleaning
  • Allow natural patina development
  • Touch up with matching lime plaster if needed
  • Maintain consistent humidity levels

Translucent Alabaster

Delicate stone requiring mineral-conscious care

  • Clean with damp microfiber cloth only
  • Use pH-neutral stone cleaner sparingly
  • Rinse thoroughly and dry immediately
  • Polish annually with natural stone wax
  • Avoid acidic cleaners that etch surface
  • Handle with care to prevent chips

Living Wood

Natural timber requiring oil nourishment

  • Oil treatment every 6 months with natural oils
  • Dust with microfiber cloth along grain
  • Maintain 30-50% humidity levels
  • Sand lightly and re-oil if surface dulls
  • Protect from direct sunlight exposure
  • Allow wood to breathe and age naturally

Artisan Glass

Optical clarity requiring precise care

  • Clean weekly with streak-free glass cleaner
  • Use lint-free cloths for polishing
  • Remove fingerprints immediately
  • Check mounting security during cleaning
  • Handle with extreme care during maintenance
  • Inspect for stress fractures regularly

Architectural Concrete

Structural material with honest aging

  • Seal annually with penetrating concrete sealer
  • Clean with mild alkaline cleaner only
  • Avoid acidic cleaners that damage surface
  • Allow natural weathering and patina
  • Brush away surface dust regularly
  • Embrace honest material character changes

Living Brass

Metal that develops beautiful patina over time

  • Polish monthly with brass cleaner for shine
  • Embrace natural patina development
  • Apply protective wax coating quarterly
  • Buff gently with soft cotton cloth
  • Remove water spots immediately
  • Allow aging process for character development

Best Practices

Do's and Don'ts

Do's

  • Turn off power before any maintenance
  • Use natural, pH-neutral cleaners for organic materials
  • Test cleaning products on hidden areas first
  • Allow natural materials to breathe and age gracefully
  • Clean with gentle, circular motions
  • Follow material-specific care instructions
  • Document maintenance dates and observations
  • Embrace natural character development
  • Use soft natural bristle brushes
  • Apply protective treatments as recommended

Don'ts

  • Never use harsh chemicals on natural materials
  • Don't use abrasive cleaners on plaster or alabaster
  • Avoid soaking wood or porous materials
  • Don't seal natural materials unnecessarily
  • Never use steel wool or scouring pads
  • Don't expose to extreme temperature changes
  • Avoid cleaning when fixture is warm from use
  • Don't use generic cleaners without testing first
  • Never spray cleaners directly on electrical parts
  • Don't resist natural patina development

Problem Solving

Troubleshooting Guide

Natural material appears dry or cracked

Cause: Low humidity, excessive heat, or insufficient nourishment for organic materials.

Solution: Increase ambient humidity to 40-60%. For wood, apply appropriate natural oil treatment. Ensure proper ventilation without drafts.

Discoloration on plaster surfaces

Cause: Natural aging, moisture exposure, or improper cleaning products.

Solution: This is often natural patina development. Clean gently with pH-neutral cleaners. If excessive, check moisture levels and ventilation. Embrace natural character changes as part of the material's beauty.

Wood surface feels rough or raised grain

Cause: Moisture exposure causing wood grain to raise, or excessive dryness.

Solution: Allow to dry completely, then lightly sand with fine grit (220+) if necessary. Apply appropriate wood oil to restore smoothness. Maintain consistent humidity levels.

LED lights flickering or dim

Cause: Incompatible dimmer, loose connections, or LED driver issues.

Solution: Check dimmer compatibility first. Verify all electrical connections are secure. If problem persists, contact a licensed electrician to inspect the LED driver and connections.

Alabaster surface developing cloudiness

Cause: Natural material porosity, improper cleaning, or mineral buildup.

Solution: Clean with specialized stone cleaner. Alabaster naturally develops character over time. Ensure proper sealing if recommended for your specific piece.

Fixture feels loose or wobbly

Cause: Loose mounting hardware or material expansion/contraction.

Solution: Turn off power and check all mounting screws. Natural materials may expand/contract seasonally. Ensure mounting system accommodates material movement.

Glass components appear cloudy

Cause: Natural mineral buildup, humidity, or cleaning residue.

Solution: Clean with distilled water and natural glass cleaner. For persistent cloudiness, use white vinegar solution. Ensure complete drying to prevent water spots.
