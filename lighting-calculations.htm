Residential Spaces

Recommended illumination levels for comfortable living environments

  • Living Room: 10-20 FC
  • Dining Room: 20-30 FC
  • Kitchen (General): 20-50 FC
  • Kitchen (Task): 50-100 FC
  • Bedroom: 10-20 FC
  • Bathroom: 20-50 FC

Commercial Spaces

Professional lighting standards for business environments

  • Office (General): 20-50 FC
  • Office (Task): 50-100 FC
  • Retail (General): 20-50 FC
  • Retail (Display): 100-200 FC
  • Restaurant: 5-20 FC
  • Hotel Lobby: 10-20 FC

Fixture Spacing

Optimal spacing guidelines for even light distribution

  • Pendant Lights: 30-36"
  • Chandelier Height: 30-34"
  • Sconce Height: 60-66"
  • Track Spacing: 4-6 ft
  • Recessed Spacing: 4-6 ft
  • Under-Cabinet: 12-18"

Living Room Lighting

Create a comfortable, layered lighting scheme for relaxation and entertainment.

Chandelier/Pendant

Central focal point for ambient lighting. 30-34" above seating area.

Table Lamps

Task lighting for reading. 58-64" from floor to shade bottom.

Wall Sconces

Accent lighting to create ambiance. 60-66" mounting height.

Floor Lamps

Additional task and ambient lighting. Position for reading areas.

Dining Room Lighting

Focus on creating intimate dining atmosphere with proper task lighting.

Dining Chandelier

Primary lighting centered over table. 30-34" above table surface.

Buffet Lamps

Accent lighting for serving areas. Coordinate with chandelier style.

Wall Sconces

Ambient lighting to enhance atmosphere. Consider dimmable options.

Kitchen Lighting

Layer task, ambient, and accent lighting for both function and style.

Island Pendants

Task lighting over work surfaces. 30-36" above countertop.

Under-Cabinet

Task lighting for food preparation. LED strips work well.

Recessed Lights

General illumination. Space 4-6 feet apart for even coverage.
