Professional Standards

Lighting Guidelines

🏠

Residential Spaces

Recommended illumination levels for comfortable living environments

  • Living Room: 10-20 FC
  • Dining Room: 20-30 FC
  • Kitchen (General): 20-50 FC
  • Kitchen (Task): 50-100 FC
  • Bedroom: 10-20 FC
  • Bathroom: 20-50 FC
🏢

Commercial Spaces

Professional lighting standards for business environments

  • Office (General): 20-50 FC
  • Office (Task): 50-100 FC
  • Retail (General): 20-50 FC
  • Retail (Display): 100-200 FC
  • Restaurant: 5-20 FC
  • Hotel Lobby: 10-20 FC
📐

Fixture Spacing

Optimal spacing guidelines for even light distribution

  • Pendant Lights: 30-36"
  • Chandelier Height: 30-34"
  • Sconce Height: 60-66"
  • Track Spacing: 4-6 ft
  • Recessed Spacing: 4-6 ft
  • Under-Cabinet: 12-18"
🔆

Light Quality

Color temperature and rendering guidelines

  • Warm White: 2700K
  • Soft White: 3000K
  • Cool White: 4000K
  • CRI Minimum: 80+
  • CRI Preferred: 90+
  • R9 Value: 50+

Living Room Lighting

Create a comfortable, layered lighting scheme for relaxation and entertainment.

Chandelier/Pendant

Central focal point for ambient lighting. 30-34" above seating area.

Table Lamps

Task lighting for reading. 58-64" from floor to shade bottom.

Wall Sconces

Accent lighting to create ambiance. 60-66" mounting height.

Floor Lamps

Additional task and ambient lighting. Position for reading areas.

Dining Room Lighting

Focus on creating intimate dining atmosphere with proper task lighting.

Dining Chandelier

Primary lighting centered over table. 30-34" above table surface.

Buffet Lamps

Accent lighting for serving areas. Coordinate with chandelier style.

Wall Sconces

Ambient lighting to enhance atmosphere. Consider dimmable options.

Kitchen Lighting

Layer task, ambient, and accent lighting for both function and style.

Island Pendants

Task lighting over work surfaces. 30-36" above countertop.

Under-Cabinet

Task lighting for food preparation. LED strips work well.

Recessed Lights

General illumination. Space 4-6 feet apart for even coverage.

Bedroom Lighting

Create a relaxing atmosphere with layered lighting for comfort and functionality.

Ceiling Fixture

Central ambient lighting. Consider dimmable chandelier or pendant.

Bedside Lamps

Task lighting for reading. 24-27" from mattress to shade bottom.

Accent Lighting

Wall sconces or LED strips for ambient mood lighting.

Bathroom Lighting

Focus on task lighting around mirrors with complementary ambient illumination.

Vanity Lighting

Side-mounted sconces or linear fixture above mirror. Even face illumination.

Shower Lighting

Recessed lights rated for wet locations. Avoid shadows in shower area.

Ambient Lighting

Central fixture for general illumination. Consider decorative chandelier.

Office Lighting

Optimize for productivity with proper task lighting and minimal glare.

Desk Lighting

Adjustable task lighting from side or behind to avoid screen glare.

Overhead Lighting

Even general illumination. Avoid direct glare on computer screens.

Accent Lighting

Wall sconces or floor lamps to reduce contrast and eye strain.
