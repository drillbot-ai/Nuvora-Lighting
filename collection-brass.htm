Material Story

The Alchemy of Time & Metal

Brass is the only metal that improves with age, developing a living patina that tells the story of its environment and use. Our artisans work with high-quality brass alloys, creating fixtures that begin with bright golden warmth and evolve into rich, complex surfaces that reflect years of gentle oxidation and handling.

"Brass teaches patience—its true beauty emerges not in perfection, but in the graceful acceptance of change. Each fixture becomes a timepiece, marking moments through the slow alchemy of oxidation and touch."

We celebrate both the initial brilliance of polished brass and the anticipated patina that will develop. Each piece is designed to age beautifully, with forms that enhance rather than hide the natural aging process, creating lighting that becomes more personal and characterful over time.
